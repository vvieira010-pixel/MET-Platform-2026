[narrator] Listen to a manager speaking to new employees.
Welcome, everyone, to your first day at Greenfield Corporation. Over the next three months, you will be part of our induction programme designed to help you understand our company culture, policies, and your specific roles. Each week you'll attend training sessions on different topics, from customer service to health and safety. You'll also be assigned a mentor who will guide you through your first few months. At the end of the programme, you will receive a certificate of completion, which is a great addition to your professional portfolio. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to speak to your manager or submit an application through the HR portal for any additional support you may need.
