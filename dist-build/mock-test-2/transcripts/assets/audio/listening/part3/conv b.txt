Good afternoon, everyone. I'm pleased to announce that our city's new rapid bus transit system will begin operations next month. This service will feature dedicated bus lanes and modern stations, allowing for much faster travel times across the city. We believe this will significantly reduce congestion on our roads and provide a reliable alternative to driving. The initial phase will cover the downtown area and extend to the main residential districts. We encourage all residents to use the service and provide feedback through our website so we can continue to improve. Your input will help us plan future expansions.
