MET Mock Test – CEFR Grade Scale

Use the number of correct answers out of 50 to estimate the CEFR level.

0–16 correct  → A2 or below
17–24 correct → B1-
25–33 correct → B1 to low B2
34–40 correct → B2
41–46 correct → strong B2
47–50 correct → C1

Recommended final report format:
- Score: ___ / 50
- Estimated CEFR: ___
- Difficulty level: Easy / Medium / Hard / Very Hard

Suggested teacher interpretation:
- A2 or below: needs foundational reading and grammar work
- B1-: developing control, but inconsistent accuracy
- B1 to low B2: on the edge of independent user level
- B2: solid upper-intermediate performance
- Strong B2: close to advanced control
- C1: advanced proficiency
