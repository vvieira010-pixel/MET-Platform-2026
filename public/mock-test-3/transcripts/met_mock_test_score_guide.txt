MET Mock Test Score Guide by Difficulty

Section A – Grammar (20 questions)
- Easy: 1 point each
- Total: 20 points

Section B – Single-text Reading (15 questions)
- Easy: 1 point each
- Total: 15 points

Section C – Multiple-text Reading (15 questions)
- Medium: 1 point each
- Total: 15 points

Total Raw Score: 50 points

Difficulty Bands
- Easy: 0–16 correct
- Medium: 17–33 correct
- Hard: 34–42 correct
- Very Hard: 43–50 correct

Suggested interpretation
- 0–16: Pre-B1 to low B1
- 17–33: B1 to low B2
- 34–42: B2
- 43–50: strong B2 to C1

Teacher use
- 45–50: Excellent mastery
- 38–44: Strong performance
- 30–37: Developing performance
- Below 30: Needs more reading and grammar practice
